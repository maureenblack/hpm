Prosperity Counseling

Faith-based guidance for financial, spiritual, and relational well-being

Our Counseling Approach

Holistic guidance that integrates biblical wisdom with practical solutions.

At Holistic Prosperity Ministry, we believe that true prosperity encompasses every aspect of your life. Our counseling services are designed to help you align your financial decisions with your faith values while addressing the spiritual and emotional aspects of your prosperity journey.

Our certified counselors combine biblical principles with professional expertise to provide:

  • Personalized financial guidance rooted in Scripture
  • Spiritual direction for life's challenging decisions
  • Tools for breaking generational patterns of financial dysfunction
  • Support for couples navigating financial disagreements
  • Strategies for aligning your resources with your God-given purpose

"For I know the plans I have for you, plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future." - Jeremiah 29:11

Request an Appointment

Take the first step toward financial and spiritual alignment

Our counseling process begins with a complimentary 15-minute consultation to understand your needs and match you with the right counselor. All sessions are confidential and can be conducted in-person at our ministry center or virtually.

Session Length

Initial sessions are 90 minutes, with follow-up sessions of 60 minutes

Fee Structure

Sliding scale based on income, with scholarships available. No one is turned away due to financial constraints.

Availability

Daytime, evening, and weekend appointments to accommodate your schedule

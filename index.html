Our Mission

At Holistic Prosperity Ministry, we believe that true prosperity encompasses all areas of life - spiritual, financial, relational, and communal. Under the leadership of Pastor Numfor Prosper, our approach is based on the belief that prosperity flows through people to transform communities, not just for personal gain.

Our Core Values

Guiding principles that shape our ministry and community impact

Faith

We believe in the power of faith as the foundation for all aspects of life, including financial stewardship.

Stewardship

We teach responsible management of all resources entrusted to us by God.

Empowerment

We equip individuals with knowledge and skills to achieve financial independence and spiritual growth.

Integrity

Love

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Pastor Numfor Prosper, Holistic Prosperity Ministry was born from a vision to bridge spiritual growth with financial empowerment.

Pastor Numfor's journey began when he witnessed many faithful believers struggling financially despite their spiritual devotion. He recognized that many traditional religious teachings separated financial matters from spiritual growth, creating a disconnect that left many people of faith unprepared for economic challenges.

Drawing from his background in both theology and finance, Pastor Numfor developed a unique approach that integrates biblical principles with practical financial education. What started as a small Bible study group has grown into a thriving ministry that has transformed thousands of lives.

Today, Holistic Prosperity Ministry stands as a beacon of hope, demonstrating that prosperity in all areas of life - spiritual, financial, relational, and communal - is not only possible but is part of God's plan for His people.

Our Mission

To empower individuals through biblical teachings, practical financial education, and community transformation initiatives, creating a holistic approach to prosperity that honors God and serves humanity.

  • Provide sound biblical teaching on prosperity
  • Equip people with practical financial skills
  • Create opportunities for community transformation
  • Foster spiritual growth alongside financial wisdom

Our Vision

To create a global community where individuals experience abundant life through the integration of spiritual principles and financial wisdom, resulting in transformed lives, families, and communities.

  • A world where faith and finance work in harmony
  • Communities transformed by the overflow of prosperity
  • Generations equipped with both spiritual and financial literacy
  • A legacy of holistic prosperity that impacts the world

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Faith

We believe in the power of faith as the foundation for all aspects of life, including financial stewardship. Our teachings are rooted in biblical principles that have stood the test of time.

Stewardship

We teach responsible management of all resources entrusted to us by God. This includes time, talents, treasures, and relationships, recognizing that we are managers, not owners.

Empowerment

We equip individuals with knowledge and skills to achieve financial independence and spiritual growth. We believe in teaching people to fish rather than simply giving them fish.

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our teachings and practices. Transparency, honesty, and accountability are non-negotiable values that permeate everything we do.

Love

We serve with compassion, recognizing that true prosperity includes caring for others and our communities. We believe that prosperity flows through people to bless others, not just for personal gain.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our ministry

Our approach to prosperity is holistic, encompassing spiritual, financial, relational, and communal well-being. We believe that true prosperity flows through people to transform communities, not just for personal gain. We integrate biblical principles with practical financial education to help individuals achieve balanced prosperity that honors God and serves humanity.

There are many ways to get involved with our ministry! You can attend our weekly services, join one of our small groups, volunteer for our community outreach programs, enroll in our CrypStock Prosperity Academy, or become a financial partner. Visit our "Get Involved" page for more information or contact us directly to find the best fit for your gifts and interests.

The CrypStock Prosperity Academy is our flagship financial education program that teaches biblical principles of wealth creation alongside practical skills in traditional and modern investments, including stocks, cryptocurrency, and other financial instruments. The academy offers courses for all levels, from basic financial literacy to advanced investment strategies, all within a faith-based framework.

While our primary focus is on education and empowerment rather than direct financial assistance, we do have community support programs for those in need. Our Love in Action Fellowship provides temporary assistance to families facing financial hardship, and we offer scholarships for our educational programs. We believe in equipping people with the tools to achieve long-term financial independence.

Our ministry is funded through a combination of tithes and offerings from our congregation, tuition from educational programs (with scholarship options available), partnerships with like-minded organizations and businesses, and donations from supporters. We practice financial transparency and publish annual impact reports detailing how funds are used to further our mission.

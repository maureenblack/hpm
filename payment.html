Payment FAQs

Answers to common questions about our payment process

Yes, all payment information is encrypted and processed securely. We use industry-standard security protocols to protect your data. We do not store complete credit card information on our servers.

Recurring payments can be set up using credit/debit cards or PayPal. During the payment process, simply select the "Make this recurring" option and choose your preferred frequency (monthly, quarterly, or annual). For payment methods that don't support automatic recurring payments (MoMo, Zelle, CashApp, Bank Transfer), you can sign up for payment reminders.

Yes, you will receive an email receipt for all payments. For ministry donations, you will also receive a tax receipt that can be used for tax deduction purposes. You can also create an account to access your payment history and receipts at any time.

For manual payment methods (MoMo, Zelle, CashApp, Bank Transfer), you will receive a reference code during the payment process. Include this code with your payment. Once we verify the payment, you will receive a confirmation email. This process typically takes 1-2 business days. You can also contact our finance team at finance@holisticprosperityministry.org for verification.

Yes, most CrypStock Academy courses offer payment plan options. You can choose to pay in full or in monthly installments. The specific payment plan options will be displayed during the course selection process. We also offer scholarships for qualified individuals. Contact our education team at education@holisticprosperityministry.org for more information.

Love in Action Fellowship

Building meaningful relationships for spiritual and financial growth

Our Fellowship Philosophy

We believe that true prosperity happens in community.

The Love in Action Fellowship is built on the biblical principle that believers are meant to grow together, support one another, and hold each other accountable. Our small groups provide a safe space for authentic relationships where members can:

  • Apply biblical prosperity principles in daily life
  • Receive prayer and encouragement during financial challenges
  • Share wisdom and experiences about stewardship
  • Build lasting friendships with like-minded believers
  • Celebrate financial and spiritual victories together

Our fellowship groups meet weekly in homes, coffee shops, and at our ministry center to study Scripture, pray together, and support one another's prosperity journey.

"Where two or three gather in my name, there am I with them." - Matthew 18:20

How to Join a Fellowship Group

Becoming part of our fellowship community is easy!

1

Fill out the interest form

Complete the form with your contact information and fellowship group preferences.

2

Meet with a fellowship coordinator

A brief 15-minute call to discuss your interests and answer any questions.

3

Attend your first meeting

You'll be welcomed by the group leader and introduced to your new fellowship family.

4

Commit to regular participation

Experience the full benefits of fellowship through consistent engagement.

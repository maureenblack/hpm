CrypStock Prosperity Academy

Faith-Based Financial Education for Generational Wealth

Our Philosophy & Approach

Biblical Foundation

Our curriculum integrates biblical principles of stewardship, generosity, and wisdom with practical financial education.

Holistic Approach

We address the spiritual, emotional, and practical aspects of money management for true financial freedom.

Generational Impact

Our goal is to break cycles of poverty and financial illiteracy to create lasting generational wealth.

Program Offerings

Comprehensive education to build financial literacy and wealth

Biblical Financial Principles

This foundational course explores what the Bible teaches about money, stewardship, and financial wisdom. Students learn how to apply timeless biblical principles to modern financial challenges.

  • 8-week course
  • Tuesdays, 7-9pm
  • Main Campus & Online
Learn More

Stock Market Literacy

Demystifying the stock market for beginners, this program teaches the fundamentals of investing, market analysis, and building a values-aligned portfolio for long-term growth.

  • 10-week course
  • Thursdays, 6:30-8:30pm
  • Financial Center & Online
Learn More

Cryptocurrency Fundamentals

This innovative program introduces blockchain technology, cryptocurrency markets, and responsible digital asset investing with a focus on risk management and long-term strategy.

  • 6-week course
  • Saturdays, 10am-12pm
  • Tech Center & Online
Learn More

Entrepreneurship

From idea to launch, this comprehensive program guides aspiring entrepreneurs through business planning, marketing, operations, and faith-based business ethics for sustainable success.

  • 12-week intensive
  • Wednesdays, 6-9pm
  • Business Center
